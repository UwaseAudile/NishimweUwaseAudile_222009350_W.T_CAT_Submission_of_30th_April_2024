Rental Process:

How do I rent equipment from RentTools?

Renting equipment from RentTools is easy! Simply browse our inventory, select the items you need, choose your rental period, and proceed to checkout. Once your rental is confirmed, you can choose to have the equipment delivered to your location or pick it up from one of our convenient rental centers.
