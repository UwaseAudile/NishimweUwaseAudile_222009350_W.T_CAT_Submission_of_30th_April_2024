Payment Methods

Accepted Payment Methods: We accept a variety of payment options to make renting easy and convenient for you.

Examples: Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers.

Security: Your payments are processed securely using industry-standard encryption methods to protect your financial information.

Payment Authorization: Your payment method will be authorized for the rental amount upon checkout and charged upon equipment return or at the end of the rental period.

Refund Policy: Refunds for unused rental periods or canceled orders are processed according to our refund policy, which may vary based on the circumstances of each transaction.
